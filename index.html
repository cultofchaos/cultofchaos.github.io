Welcome to Cult of Chaos

A group of dedicated FOSS developers & reverse engineers.

Who We Are

The Cult of Chaos is an indie collective focused on creating free and open source software using KISS principles.

Our Values:

  • Curiosity: Always seeking to learn more and expand our skills.
  • Collaboration: Working together as a community to achieve common goals.
  • Transparency: Sharing ideas openly so that everyone can benefit from them.
  • Innovation: Pushing boundaries and embracing change to create cutting-edge solutions.

Current Projects

Here are some of the open source projects we are currently working on:

  • Chaotic Reverse Engineering Framework (CREF): An advanced RE framework designed to simplify binary analysis and modification.
  • Kek: Keylogger
